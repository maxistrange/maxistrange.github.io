MAXI STRANGE on a trip through wild oceans and emotions.
For a long period of time, songs came into existence. Now they want to leave their hometown and discover the world. Maxi Strange lives through them every night and invites everyone, who have ears for the unheard. Less is more is the maxime: The music is evoked only by vocals, drums and guitar. The songs play with progressions from soft acoustic guitar to hard riffs and from giving comfort to courage. Man can lose himself in finding words about the music - better lose yourself in the music.
